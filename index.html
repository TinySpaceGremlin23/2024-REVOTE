The 2024 Election: A Betrayal of America

The 2024 election is not just a political event—it is a betrayal of the American people. A betrayal that runs deep, that shakes the very core of our democracy. Over 20 million votes have gone unaccounted for. Millions of ballots are missing, broken machines failed in critical states, and entire districts were subjected to intimidation, interference, and destruction. Voting stations were attacked, ballot boxes were burnt, and the integrity of the entire process was sabotaged. This was no accident. This was deliberate.

Before the election, Donald J. Trump said it all: "I don’t need your votes. I have all the votes I need." He said it out loud, for the whole world to hear. He knew he would win. He knew how it would unfold. This wasn’t just arrogance—it was a warning. He had the system rigged, and we all watched it happen. From the moment he uttered those words, we knew the deck was stacked against us. The election was already decided in his favor before the people ever voted.

Kamala Harris, with her characteristic grace, tried to comfort us when the results came in. She spoke of acceptance, of dealing with the disappointment, and though her words were kind and sincere, America cannot be asked to simply accept what happened. We can’t just be disappointed anymore. We are angry. We are furious. America weeps is rage. The people of this nation have been betrayed. We were told our votes mattered, but what we’ve seen is a system built on lies. The votes that should have counted didn’t. The people who trusted the process were let down. Their voices were silenced.

The stories are everywhere—voters who watched their ballots vanish from the system, machines that malfunctioned, ballot boxes that mysteriously disappeared, and polling stations targeted by threats. All of this while millions of people who voted early, by mail, or in person have found out their votes were lost, miscounted, or never even recorded. How many more injustices do we have to witness before the truth comes out? How many more reports of stolen ballots, of corrupted machines, of deliberate sabotage? This is not a democracy. This is a sham.

Kamala Harris did what she could to calm the storm, but the people are not simply sad. We are enraged. The anger is rising, and it’s not just because of the loss, but because of the betrayal we’ve witnessed. We were told our votes mattered. We were lied to. America has been cheated, and now it is time for us to stand up and demand what’s rightfully ours.

We demand a recount. We demand a revote. A fair, transparent, and honest election—one where the people’s voices are truly heard. This isn’t just about the future of a political party; this is about the future of America itself. This is about the future of our democracy. We will not stand down. We will not stay silent.

This site exists to provide a platform for those who have been ignored, for those who have been silenced. We will not rest until the truth is revealed, until every vote is counted, and until the American people get the justice they deserve. We will not let this go. We will not let this stolen election become the new normal. The time for action is now.

America is seething with rage. We are furious. We are demanding accountability. The election was rigged, and we will not allow it to stand. The people will rise—and we will be heard.
